About Us

Praxis is a community-driven platform dedicated to preserving and sharing the art of craftsmanship. We connect master artisans with those eager to learn, repair, and create, ensuring that traditional skills are passed down through generations.

The idea for Praxis was born from a simple, frustrating experience: trying to get a beloved pair of leather boots resoled and discovering the last local cobbler had retired. This personal search revealed a much larger problem: foundational craft skills are disappearing, leaving us reliant on a culture of disposable, mass-produced goods that fill our landfills. We saw an opportunity not just to mourn this loss, but to reverse it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with a craft?

Getting started is easy! Browse our Trade-in-a-Box kits to find a craft that interests you. Each kit comes with all the tools, materials, and step-by-step video tutorials you need to begin your journey. We recommend starting with our beginner-friendly kits.

How do artisans become certified?

Our certification process involves a thorough review of an artisan's portfolio, technical skills, and commitment to quality craftsmanship. Applicants must demonstrate mastery of their craft. This ensures customers receive only the highest quality handmade goods.

What is your repair process?

Simply submit photos of your item through our online form. Our team will assess the repair needs and provide a quote. Once approved, you can mail your item to our facility. Our certified artisans will restore your item using traditional techniques and quality materials.
